Drata introduced Multiple Risk Registers in Risk Management Pro, letting organizations structure risk management by regions, products, or business units while maintaining unified visibility. The feature supports separate registers with individual owners, flexible workspace mapping, and consolidated reporting across all registers through centralized scoring and categories.
